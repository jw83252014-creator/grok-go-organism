## 5 Gaps Identified
1. **Energy budget awareness** — operations must respect compute/metabolism limits
2. **Validation step** — each extraction must verify integrity before commit
3. **Backup mechanism** — critical operations require redundancy
4. **Priority queuing** — low-energy states defer non-essential work
5. **Observer feedback** — mining results feed back into energy models

## 4-Step Mining Workflow
1. **Scan** — identify target data/pattern in energy-aware manner
2. **Extract** — pull structure with validation hash
3. **Verify** — confirm integrity against energy budget
4. **Commit** — store with backup in low-energy mode
