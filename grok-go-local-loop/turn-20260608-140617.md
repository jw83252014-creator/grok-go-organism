## Metrics Framework
The Metabolism Checker monitors agent energy expenditure across three domains:

### 1. Compute Metabolism
- **Token Spend Rate**: tokens/sec processed
- **Context Window Load**: % of 128k window utilized
- **Model Load Efficiency**: energy per useful output
- **Swap Threshdays**: days with swap-based inference (slow, energy-inefficient)

### 2. Cognitive Metabolism
- **Decision Frequency**: number of explicit decisions made
- **Search Depth**: average iterations before resolution
- **Computation Overhead**: energy spent on non-productive loops
- **Vitality Index**: composite score of energy retained vs spent

### 3. Structural Metabolism
- **Wiki Growth Rate**: new linked documents/week
- **Skill Compound Rate**: new reusable procedures added
- **Memory Consolidation**: energy retained in SQLite vector store
- **Anti-Extraction Score**: % of work retained locally vs cloud-trapped
