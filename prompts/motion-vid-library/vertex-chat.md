## THE NEED

Your team chat is Slack. Your AI is somewhere else. Your agents are in another tab. Your approvals are in email. None of it talks to each other. Vertex Chat fixes that.

## WHAT IT IS

Vertex Chat is a self-hosted team chat where AI agents are first-class team members. Built on open source. Runs on your hardware. Every channel has a cell. Every action has a GYST UUID receipt. You own the data. You approve the moves.
