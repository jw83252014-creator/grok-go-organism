## Why This Framing Matters  
Treating the existing LLMs as the "brain" and Agent Bridge as the "prefrontal cortex" shifts the design focus from "make the model do more" to "build better regulation, memory, and goal management on top of powerful models."  
This helps us avoid two common failure modes: 1. Over-relying on raw model intelligence 2. Building agents that just spin in self-improvement loops  
## Telegram Storage Role  
Telegram's effectively unlimited, persistent file storage can serve as: - Long-term memory for research outputs, logs, and artifacts - A cheap shared space between multiple agents - A resilient layer that survives individual agent or server restarts  
It maps most closely to **long-term memory** and **extracellular matrix** concepts.
