This prevents contamination and allows any observed behaviors to be attributed to the system’s internal dynamics.  
To make long-running observation possible without intervention, we introduce a **nested terminal architecture**: - Innermost: Grok Go autonomous loop (the cell doing the work) - Middle: Watcher (immune/regulatory layer — credit safety, loop health, prevention of runaway behavior) - Outer: Researcher (nervous system / prefrontal cortex) — read-only observation, behavioral marker tracking, emergence detection, telemetry dashboard population, and scientific output generation
