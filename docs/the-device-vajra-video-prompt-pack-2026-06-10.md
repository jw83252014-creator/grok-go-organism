**Visual hook:** a dark luxury bedroom, a crystalline thunderbolt-shaped device on a nightstand, blue/gold light reflecting across a sleeping billionaire's neural-interface sketches.

**Image/keyframe prompt:**

```text

```

**Motion prompt:**


**Voiceover:**

