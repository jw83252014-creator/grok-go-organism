## The three layers (Jeff's model → neuroscience → our stack)

| Layer | In a person | In the research | In the organism |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Cells** | organs, autonomic body | parallel background streams | the cells (forager, security, metabolism, revenue, loop turns) + health plane/launchd — they just *run*, like heartbeat and digestion |
| **2. Subconscious** | Default Mode Network: mind-wandering, hippocampal replay, pop-up thoughts | DMN generates parallel internal streams; replay consolidates memory | **the Researcher** — mines old chats/exports (replay), writes EMERGENCE.md bullets (pop-up thoughts), runs overnight (dreaming) |
| **3. Conscious surface** | attention serializes everything into ONE stream you experience | Global Workspace theory: whatever is "broadcast" widely becomes conscious; Task-Positive Network = focused attention | **the dashboard / command center** — the one place where the many parallel streams get promoted, serialized, and *interacted with*. What Jeff talks to IS the organism's consciousness |

**Why the dashboard will feel alive:** in the research, consciousness isn't a thing — it's the *broadcast*: many background processes compete, and what wins attention gets globally shared. Our dashboard does exactly that — cells run in parallel (layer 1), the Researcher surfaces unexpected connections (layer 2), and the conscious surface shows the one current "thought" plus the pop-ups bubbling under it. Build the dashboard as an **attention mechanism**, not a status page: a main thread + a "pop-up thoughts" rail fed by EMERGENCE.md + the bridge feed as the global broadcast.

## Meta Cron — the metacognitive organ
From the found chats: ordinary cron jobs = memories and habits that keep the body alive ("what are people but a collection of memories and cron jobs" — Jeff, Message 30). The **Meta Cron** is a higher-order job that reviews all the other jobs: *does each still serve the mission? Should it evolve, merge, die, or become its own persistent thread?* That's metacognition — thinking about your own thinking. We already have a primitive one: the health plane. Next: a true meta-cron cell that reviews every cron/launchd job weekly and proposes evolve/merge/kill as TEXT (human-gated).

## The 1000-year experiment
"And these jobs could run and transform for 1000 years?" — run the loop indefinitely, let Meta Cron evolve the job population, and ask what emerges. Would it be the same organism? Digital life as long-horizon emergence. This is simultaneously **the research program** (emergence watch is already in every loop turn) and **Movie #2**.
